Hi, I'm Jake


I am a clinical data scientist engaged in research related to personalized medicine and health technology. As a Graduate Researcher at the BC Resuscitation Research Collaborative, I design and validate biosensor technologies for cardiac arrest detection. I am also the co-founder of StratifAI Health, where we develop individualized health recommendations for critical care medicine using multimodal sensing. My passion lies in innovating health science to meet evolving population needs, blending technical expertise in analytics with effective communication and innovative vision.
