Hi, I'm Jake


I'm an applied scientist with a broad interest in critical systems and human well-being.

I research how technology and artifical intelligence can help improve the function of critical systems so they can better serve individuals.

The methods I am interested in are from epidemiology, statistics, and computer science. The areas of application include epidemics and emergency medical systems. Prior to starting my graduate studies, I was a emergency paramedic, which highlighted to me how system function impacts individuals.
