Hi, I'm Jake


I am a clinical data scientist engaged in research related to personalized medicine and health technology. As a Graduate Researcher at the BC Resuscitation Research Collaborative, I lead the design and validation of biosensor technologies for cardiac arrest detection. I am also the co-founder of StratifAI Health, where we develop individualized health recommendations using multimodal sensing. My passion lies in innovating health science to meet evolving population needs, blending technical expertise in statistical analysis with effective communication.
